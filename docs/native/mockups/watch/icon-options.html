Recommended split (dual-asset, Apple-standard pattern):
  • iPhone: Candidate A · wordmark "faff" (italic Bebas Neue, orange gradient). iPhone icons display at 60×60+ pt — wordmark reads cleanly.
  • Watch: Candidate B · italic "f" monogram. Single-glyph density matches Apple Watch home grid rhythm and stays legible at 24pt.
  • Reject C: stride pictogram is too close to Apple Workout. Brand-recognition risk outweighs Apple-native fit.
  • Reject D: capital "F" loses the italic-wordmark brand link. Bebas Neue lowercase italic is part of the brand identity (faff-topbar-logo on web).

Awaiting David's pick. If the recommendation lands, the SwiftUI implementation builds both assets and uses Asset Catalog to route per-platform.
