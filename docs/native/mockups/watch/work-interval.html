Design notes · this is the most-glanced screen in the entire app:
  • Information hierarchy: target pace (medium) above · current pace (huge, color-coded) is the focal element · delta is a precision signal
  • Color coding: green = within tolerance band · amber = drifting (>10s/mi delta sustained 5+ sec) · red = sustained >15s/mi (see edge-states/pace-drift.html)
  • "3 / 6" in the top right gives spatial context · which rep am I on
  • Time remaining in interval (1:37) is the second-most-important number · runner uses this to pace breathing/effort to the finish
  • No distance or elapsed time shown · deliberately omitted · the rep ends on time, not distance · less to read at speed
  • No HR target · per scoping decision (HR-zone-based targeting deferred) · HR shown as informational only
  • 2-second glance: target on top, current in the middle, time remaining bottom · eyes scan top-to-bottom in 1-2 seconds
