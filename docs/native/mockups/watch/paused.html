Design notes:
  • Triggered by side-button press (Apple HIG convention for workout pause)
  • Amber color · paused = warning state, not error · the workout is intact, just frozen
  • Time + context shown · "in Interval 3/6 · frozen at 6:31 target" · runner knows exactly where they were when they paused
  • Two big buttons side by side · Resume (green) / End (red) · 44pt+ tap targets for cold/wet/gloved hands
  • "Skip this interval →" · the manual-lap override from the scoping doc · runner can bail on a bad rep without ending the whole workout
  • Pause itself does NOT trigger a haptic · the user initiated it, they know it happened
