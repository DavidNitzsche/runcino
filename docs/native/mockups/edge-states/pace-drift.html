Design notes (v2) · screen-filling state, instant recognition:
  • Tinted background · amber/red wash on the whole screen · runner sees "warning" before reading any number · light tint in light mode, deeper in dark mode (preserves OLED battery)
  • Current pace at 92pt · fills 60% of the vertical space · this is the focal element during drift · no dead middle zone
  • Header is the warning · "⚠ DRIFTING · 2/6" + delta chip ("+15s") · spatial context (which rep) + severity in 0.5 seconds
  • Target as a thin strip · "TARGET 6:31" small + horizontal · the target is reference, not focal · runner already knows it
  • Action verb on red · "PUSH IT" · brief, imperative · the runner can ignore but the cue is clear
  • Footer bigger · 22pt time-remaining is now meaningful · runner uses this to decide effort allocation
  • Haptic cadence unchanged · subtle amber haptic at sustained 5s · stronger red haptic at sustained 10s · visual reinforces, not replaces
