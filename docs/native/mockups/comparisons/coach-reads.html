Deferred from iPhone v1

Coach Reads is the deepest review surface in the system. Five findings · evidence · falsifier · math · all stacked into one card the runner pores over before/after a workout.

Why iPhone defers it:

  • Long-form on a small screen feels wrong. This card is 2-3 scroll-heights tall on web. On iPhone it would either truncate (loses meaning) or scroll endlessly (loses reading flow).
  • Daily-touch vs. deep-work. Per the watch-primary framing, iPhone is the bridge: today's workout, sync status, race countdown. Deep VDOT analysis belongs on a screen where the runner sits down with intent.
  • The data is the same. The tier-1 endpoints (/api/adaptive/vdot-verdict, /api/health/z2-sparkline, etc.) are all reachable from iPhone — when iPhone needs them. v0 just doesn't surface them.

What iPhone does surface from this data:

  • Current VDOT shown in Settings (read-only, with "edit on web" footnote)
  • Race trajectory state on the Race detail screen (V3 · ahead/on-track/behind)
  • Suspended signals notice when injury is marked (edge state)

If David finds he needs Coach Reads on iPhone in real use, we add it — but the default scope is web-only, deliberately.
