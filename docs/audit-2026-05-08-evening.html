Make the plan trustworthy

Runcino · Production
Guiding principle
The engine must be rooted in research AND in your actual data — your runs, paces, HR, Strava history, recovery scores. Generic doctrine is the floor; your data is the ceiling. The plan should never override what's actually happening in your training to follow a textbook.
