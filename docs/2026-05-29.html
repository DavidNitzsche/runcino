SHIPPED · flatten

Card chrome → spec-sheet ledger

The recipe, applied surface by surface: rounded-card wrappers become hairline-ruled ledger rows — a 1px top rule, transparent ground, no radius, content flush to the column. The discipline — a gut you can trust, not a rewrite

Every commit in the sweep is token-only: no logic, JSX, props, copy, or behaviour changed. That's what kept a ~40-file pass safe enough to push straight to main. The risk in a mechanical sweep is over-converting — so four guardrails decided what to leave hardcoded:

LEAVE  dark ink on a saturated/accent fill   (#0e1014 on var(--race)) — correct both skins
LEAVE  hex-alpha concatenation               (${color}30) — var(--x)30 is invalid CSS
LEAVE  white / pastel text over a gradient   (StatTrio on the poster wash)
LEAVE  literal colour-picker swatches + HR-zone fills (user data, not theme)

And the revert surface from yesterday is unchanged: pull data-skin="paper" off <html> on web, flip the 16 Theme.* aliases + the .preferredColorScheme line on iOS. The sweep moved values onto tokens — it didn't add a single new branch.
