You're in week 1 of BASE — the rhythm that'll carry you through to race day. This week sits at 43.8 miles, just a tick above last week, and Tuesday brings the first quality session: Cruise Intervals. That's intentional. We're building aerobic horsepower before the sharper work arrives in BUILD.

You're 83 days out. The plan is about laying the foundation — aerobic base, durability, getting your body ready for the intensity that's coming. Sunday's long run (12.1 mi) is the anchor. Everything else supports it.
