FAFF.RUN Settings redesign · 2026-06-12
The problem

Settings does not let you set the things the coach needs

The data model already stores almost everything. The screens do not surface it. There are two settings systems on web and two on iPhone, they contradict each other, and most of the controls render but write nowhere. The fix is consolidation and wiring, not a rebuild. One settings surface per platform, every row backed by the database, every plan-shaping value read back by the coach.

What you get today
  • Units toggle that resets on reload and controls nothing
  • Long-run day, rest day, briefing time shown as hardcoded text you cannot change
  • Notification toggles that are fake state and persist nothing
  • Name, height, weight, sex, birthday set once at onboarding, no edit path
  • Days per week not editable anywhere after onboarding
  • Account email hardcoded into the view, not read from your profile
  • Timezone auto-set once, never updates, no way to see or change it
  • A second real settings form hidden behind an avatar on the docs pages
What the redesign gives you
  • One screen, eight clear groups, every row writes to the DB
  • Training group reshapes the plan: days per week, long-run day, rest day, quality days, weekly target
  • Weight added as a real field with HealthKit auto-fill
  • Timezone auto-detects, updates when you travel, manual override wins
  • Physiology you can actually edit: LTHR and max-HR drive your zones
  • The dead units toggle removed until the display layer is real
  • Real notification controls, quiet hours, daily briefing time
  • Real account row, working sign-out, accent picker kept
