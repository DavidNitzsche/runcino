Plain Language & UX Polish

2026-05-22 · Governing directive: "make this so me and any normal person knows wtf any of this means. NORMAL LANGUAGE everywhere," "don't cite sources anywhere," and "be present — way less text." This pass swept jargon and citations off every screen, made the coach say something worth reading, and cut the noise.

The one-line story

The engine was right; the words were wrong. Screens leaked VDOT, HRR, VO₂max, CTL/ATL/TSB, ACWR, "Research/… §…", and walls of explanatory text. Now every rendered string is normal English, the coach lines cite real numbers instead of restating the obvious, and the Today view is title + stats + buttons. 16 commits, all live.

Plain language & citations

  • Citations gone from every rendered surface — onboarding, edit-profile, the NextPush card, the "Coach updated" card, plan workout descriptions, the training adaptations feed, stale-fitness and post-marathon notes. (The citation data field is kept; it's just never rendered.)
  • Jargon → everyday words: VDOT → "fitness score" (number kept), HRR/Karvonen/Daniels dropped from HR-zone copy, run recap shows "% effort" not "% HRR", ACWR 1.05 → "load steady/building/high", form chip "OVERREACHED" → "Running tired", workout descriptions lose WU/CD/MP/LT2/Pfitzinger/Norwegian.
  • "Why this run" rebuilt as 3 plain lines (what · where in plan · the point), then — per the latest note — removed entirely from the Today view.

Coach voice — insight, not narration

You said it was BORING — it just restated what you can already see. Every clause now cites a real number and gives a forward call.

Before: "Your body is absorbing this block really well — fitness is stacking up right on schedule."

After: "Mileage is up 14% over the last month and you've kept 82% of it easy — that's exactly how fitness compounds without breaking you. Don't touch the formula."

Stale long-run / quality lines now name your configured days, not a hardcoded "Saturday/Tuesday." All numbers come from existing data.

Behavior fixes

  • Weekly-mileage insight — "41 of 41 mi this week" no longer shows mid-week. The window is a rolling last-7-days, so the on-plan / short call now waits until the week is essentially done.
  • Check-in confirmed state — logging flips the card to a solid green card with a checkmark + your values + Edit, on web overview, web health, and iPhone.
  • "Coach updated" dismissal persists server-side (a new seen status) — clear it on iPhone and it stays cleared on the web. It was localStorage-only.

Layout & interaction

  • Recent races are clickable into their recap; Upcoming Races is a clean vertical list instead of the hard-to-scan timeline.
  • Course-profile elevation is filled with each phase's difficulty color as a soft gradient (clipped to the curve), replacing the flat grey fill + separate band.
  • Today view stripped down — removed the why-expander, the pace-guidance paragraph, the pre-workout briefing, and the Z2 stimulus block. Title, stats, buttons. Present.
