Decide · or mix

Pick a hub
language.

G, H, and I are the three B+C hybrids. Each puts training and health at the centre and lets the race be one module. If one of them is close but not quite, say what to move: bigger masthead, smaller tiles, more paper, less ink, tighter grid, looser grid.

  • I · Poster HubThe original. Poster tile + small metric tiles. The shape you liked.
  • J · Stats WallPoster + 12-cell dense stat grid. Month · year · PRs. Most information per square inch.
  • K · Record BookPoster + typographic ledger of PRs in roman numerals. Reads like Vol. III of your running.
  • L · CalendarPoster + 365-day heatmap + headline stat tiles. See every run. Streaks become the page.

G, H are the earlier hub variants without big stats. A–F are still on the table as race-day or post-race views once the hub is picked. And any of J/K/L can be mixed — e.g. poster + stats wall + heatmap stacked vertically.
