Notes for the /web/ port

How this plugs in

Every page reads a userHasAnyConnector and userHasAnyRun derived flag from the store. When false: the Connect banner shows at the page top, and any data-dependent card swaps its body for the matching <EmptyZone /> component (different icon + copy + CTAs per page). When true: normal render.

The Plan, Race, Check-in form, and Coach narrative are NOT data-dependent — they come from onboarding inputs + the plan template + research doctrine. Those render in full from day one. This is what keeps the empty state from feeling like a broken app.
