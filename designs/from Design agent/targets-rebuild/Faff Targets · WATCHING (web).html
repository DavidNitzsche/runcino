Design decisions
How this pass resolves the open questions in §7 of the brief.
§7.1 · The gauge
Killed the fake needle. Replaced with an honest projection band.
A single needle implied precision the engine doesn't have. The band is a clean number line: current fitness (1:34:54) trailing the plan target (1:30:00) on the right, with the 4:54 gap being watched between them.
§2.1 · Plan-trusts-itself
The headline number stays at the goal while Watching.
1:30:00 is the hero. The raw VDOT read never takes the headline until off-track · here it lives on the band and in "the work" as honest context, not as a verdict.
§5.6 · "What moves the gauge"
Two dense paragraphs became a 3-rung status ladder.
The runner sees the rung they're on (Watching), what lifts them up, and what drops them down · the conditions are scannable instead of buried in prose.
§5.7 · VDOT was invisible
Surfaced as "the work behind the number."
VDOT 48.5, its 6-week delta, what it implies at the half, and what the goal needs (~50.5). The single most important fitness number now anchors the page that's most about fitness vs goal.
§7.4 · PRs vs goal
Re-anchored PRs to the goal; dropped block trajectory.
PRs stop being a disconnected grid · the half PR is measured straight against the goal (−3:05) so it serves the projection story. Block phase (BASE→TAPER) was removed · it already lives on Train.
§7.6 / §7.7 · Races + diagnostic
Races are a full upcoming calendar; the diagnostic folded into the band.
Every upcoming race, tagged A / tune-up, with its countdown · a real calendar, not a list of two. The clinical "Diagnostic · VDOT projects…" footer is gone · that number now lives honestly on the band.
