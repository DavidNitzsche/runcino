Phase 2 design decisions
Editorial beats, not tiles
Hero number dominates the first viewport. Course profile is the main working surface. Splits live in the rail. Nutrition below. Strategy below that. Reference last and quiet. Each beat has one job.
Pace heatmap cut from overview
Per-mile color bars belong on a race detail page, not the overview. The splits panel and phase cards give the same information at the right level of detail. The heatmap earns its space in post-race analysis.
Race orange rule locked
Race orange appears only on: race countdown (nav pill, hero eyebrow, "12 days"), the Finish split, and race-day mode indicators. Hurricane Point is a course feature, not a race-system signal. Its annotation uses --line for the dashed marker and --mute for the label. Active blue carries the elevation chart fill and caffeinated gel pills.
