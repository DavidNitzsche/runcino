Why
Lactate threshold work. The 9 miles at effort extends your ability to sustain marathon pace when it starts to hurt. One quality block per week. This is it.
Focus
Stay even through mile 5. The back half of a tempo is where the work happens. Don't push the first three miles.
Back off if
Pace requires forcing. HR won't settle below 170 after mile 2. Heat above 70°F. Drop to easy and treat it as an aerobic day.
Sends as a structured workout
Design decisions in this component
Active blue at full saturation: "Today" pill border and text, interval left-border, interval pace text. All mark the same semantic job.
Warmup and cooldown rows render in --mute on --surface-2. Structural, not informational. The tempo row earns the accent because it is the key effort.
No wash backgrounds in this component. Wash tokens are documented but not used here. They belong on calendar cell hovers and phase coach blocks.
CTA button is --ink on --bg. The blue accent is doing work on the card. The action does not compete with it.
